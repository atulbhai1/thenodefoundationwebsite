Our Mission

Why "Node"?

In computer networks, a node is a connection point that can receive, create, store, or send data. Similarly, The Node Foundation serves as a connection point between generations, facilitating the exchange of knowledge, skills, and experiences through technology.

Who We Are

We are a collective of motivated high school students passionate about expanding access to tech education and building digital confidence across all age groups. Our youth-led approach brings fresh perspectives to digital literacy while valuing the wisdom of experience from older generations.

Our Plan

We approach digital literacy through four key pillars that ensure comprehensive learning and confidence building.

N

Navigate

Helping users confidently operate devices and applications through guided practice and troubleshooting.

O

Outreach

Teaching essential cybersecurity principles and introductory coding concepts to build foundational skills.

D

Demonstrate

Providing hands-on learning with step-by-step guidance to ensure practical understanding.

E

Explain

Building long-term confidence through patient instruction and clear explanations of concepts.

Our Programs

S

Senior Division

Tailored programs for adults and seniors focusing on practical digital skills and safety.

  • Digital safety and cybersecurity fundamentals
  • Identifying and avoiding online scams
  • Social media awareness and best practices
  • Device operation and troubleshooting
J

Junior Division

Engaging tech education for younger students to build foundational skills.

  • Python programming workshops for middle schoolers
  • Introduction to basic tech concepts for elementary students
  • Creative computing with Scratch and block-based coding
  • Internet safety and responsible digital citizenship

Our Code of Conduct

These core values guide all our interactions and programs.

Respect & Empathy

We honor diverse perspectives and approach each interaction with understanding and kindness.

Professionalism

We maintain high standards in our communication, preparation, and program delivery.

Privacy & Safety

We prioritize the confidentiality and security of all participants' information and experiences.

Inclusivity & Patience

We create welcoming environments where all learners can progress at their own pace.
